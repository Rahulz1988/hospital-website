Welcome to Souparnika

We, at Souparnika Ayurveda Hospital are here to help you rediscover yourself with the ravishing and rejuvenating power of Ayurveda , the complete science of life. Ayurveda preaches to maintain the supreme gift of health by enhancing the power of life itself. It incorporates both the preventive and curative aspects and that too from the therapies and medication brought to you straight from the lap of mother nature. Thus through Ayurveda, we aim to maintain the health of the healthy, and rediscover and replace that of the diseased.

Our Medical Departments

Our Featured Services

Pre-conceptional Care

It is a newer topic to the modern world, but is of greater importance in reproductive health according to Ayurveda."Plan your pregnancy " with our expert guidance and reduce the unwanted complications during pregnancy, delivery and postpartum period.
Antenatal Care

Includes the specific diet, regimen and medications during pregnancy. Our team is also specialized ito provide counselling for pregnant women and their family which will reduce the chaos during and after pregnancy.
Postnatal Care

Includes the specfic diet, regimen and therapies for both mother and baby. We also give equal importance to the mental and physical health of the mother and provide guidance for the new parents and family.
Marma Treatments

107 Marmas or vital points are described in Ayurveda.Injuries to these vital points leads to some severe pathological conditions, eg: "Kukundara marma abhighata" cause paraplegia. Ayurvedic management protects these vital points and cure the pathological conditions caused by the trauma.




Urology

It is related with urinary system viz Kidney, Ureter, Urinary bladder, Urethra, Prostate etc and its pathological conditions along with the management.Urinary tract infection (UTI) , Renal calculi (Kidney Stone) , Renal cyst, BPH are the common pathological conditions. Ayurvedic conservative management relieve the complications and helps the patients to lead a better life.
Joint Disorders

Includes degenrative changes like Osteoarthrities, Joint deformities, Dislocations, Ligament and Tendon injuries. Ayurvedic treatments help the patients to recover from the difficulties and to conduct a normal life without complications. Our expert medical team will guide you in your journey to a painless life.
Gyneacological Disorders

Includes the treatments for all types of gynecological disorders with specialized therapies, medications, lifestyle modifications and counselling sessions. Diseases like PCOS, Endometriosis, Irregular Menstruation, Painful Menstruation etc are treated successfully through Ayurveda. Menarche, Menopause and such other reproductive milestones are made a blissful experience with expert guidance.
Infertility Treatments

Includes the therapies and medications aiming at uprooting the cause of disease an provide the patients with mental and physical support in the journey of fullfilling their dreams. We focus on a healthy family generation while giving treatments for male and female infertility.
Yoga and Fitness

Includes the personalised ayurvedic diet and lifestyle guidance and aims at the motto of "prevention is better than cure". Our experts help you to modify your lifestyle according to your health needs by incorporating Prakriti specific diet, Yoga, Pranayama sessions.
