Our Treatment Packages

Garbhadana(Pre Conceptional Care)

The preparation before conceiving your precious child according to Ayurveda comes under this treatment package. A healthy conception, takes place in healthy body only. Plan your pregnancy through Ayurveda with us to reduce the complications during pregnancy and delivery, also counselling sessions will be there to prepare you as parent.
Garbharaksha (Anti Natal Care)

Pregnancy is a period of mental and physical discomforts alongwith various anxiety and stress due to the hormonal interplay. Ayurveda has various treatment modalities based on the condition of the patient to reduce such discomforts and provide you with a fruitful and happy pregnancy. Antenatal care starts from the first month of pregnancy and extends upto delivery. Counselling sessions are also provided for preparing your family for this wonderful experience.
Prasava Raksha(Post Natal Care)

Period after delivery of your baby is the most vulnerable period for both mother and baby since the efforts made by both of them is immense. So to ease their journey together and make sure they are healthy, through the therapies of ayurveda, sutika paricharya package is being offered. When this is done under the supervision of an eminent medical team, the postpartum period becomes extra special.
Weight Loss Treatment

Obesity is a threat to the society as it causes physical mental and social I'll effects to the patient. Maintaining a healthy weight is necessary for good health. Through the weightloss package, diet and exercise regimen will be designed based on your medical and personal history alongwith medications and therapies.
Weight Gain Treatment

According to Ayurveda, similar to the obese patients, lean patients are also not a sign of good health. This malnourished state is the basic cause of various systemic illnesses also. Through the weight gain package, specific diet, regimen and medications are selected and various therapies are also used to help you attain the healthy BMI.
Sukhachikitsa Package

Ayurveda is used to cure the disease of the diseased and also to maintain the health of the healthy. As the saying goes, prevention is better than cure, the sukhachikitsa package provides you with ayurvedic ways of maintaining your health and to prevent many diseases. It aims at overall development of your mental and physical health.

Panchakarma Treatments

DHARA

In this continual pouring of warm medicated oil or a herbal decoction over the body is done. Dhara is a kind of external application of medicated liquids.

ABHYANGA

Abhyanga in Ayurveda means oil massage, trained masseurs are required to perform this therapy when it is used for disease management in ayurveda or Panchakarma.

KARNA-POORANA

Ayurvedic treatment suggested for a wide range of ear-related and cranial issues.Karnapoorana is prescribed for the treatment of ear injuries and pains, dizziness, vertigo, and nausea

KATIVASTI

Kativasti is an ayurvedic therapy to cure lower backache, stiffness, and pain felt down the back. Oil mixed with black gram dough is placed on the patient for an extended duration.

NASYA

Nasya is medication through the nose. In this treatment, the application of medicated oil or powders through the nostrils is done to prevent various illness.

NETRA TARPANA

This treatment aims to provide optimum rejuvenation to eyes. Black gram paste is put around the eye and herbal healing ghee is put in the groove which helps in bringing strength to eyes.

PODIKIZI

Picture of Podikkizhi - an Ayurvedic therapy in which the body of the patient is fomented with linen bags containing a mixture of horse gram, black gram, gingelly seeds and other vital ingredients in powdered form.

SHIROVASTI

Shirovasti, one of the Ayurvedic treatements, which is considered more of a palliative treatment of ayurveda than an eliminative one. In this process, medicated oil is poured into the sleeve and allowed to remain on the pate for a while.

SNANAM

Snana or medicated bath as part of post treatment care in Ayurveda. In Ayurvedic texts it is recommeded to have a daily bath after an Abhyanga or self-administered whole body oil massage.

UROVASTI

Urovasti is an Ayurvedic treatment similar to Kativasti were Urovasti is administered to the Uras or the chest to treat pain in the sternum area, here a reservoir is done on the chest with black gram.

TALAPOTHICHIL

Talapotichil in Ayurveda treatment involves covering of the head with medicinal paste. Talapotichil is an important treatment for many minor and major diseases that afflict the head.

SWEDANA

This process involves inducing sweat in the patient. Swedana gives relief from health problems like asthma, catarrh, hemiplegia and many other diseases caused by Vata disorders.
